Getting Started

Q: How do I create my first business book?
A: Tap the "+" button on the dashboard and select "New Book". Enter your business details and start tracking your finances.

Q: How do I invite team members?
A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Organization ‚Üí Members, then tap "Invite Member" and enter their email address.

Security & Privacy

Q: Is my financial data secure?
A: Yes! EtBook uses industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage. Your data is protected with PIN/biometric locks and encrypted transmission.

Q: Can I delete my account and data?
A: Absolutely. Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Delete Account to permanently remove all your data.

Features

Q: Can I export my financial data?
A: Yes! Use the export feature in each book to download your data as CSV or PDF for accounting purposes.

Q: Does EtBook work offline?
A: EtBook requires an internet connection for most features, including recording new transactions. However, you can view previously loaded data while offline. The app is designed as an online-first collaborative platform.

Account Deletion

Need to delete your EtBook account?

We make account deletion simple and transparent:

  1. Open the EtBook app
  2. Go to Settings Data Management
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision

What happens when you delete your account:

  • All your financial data is permanently deleted
  • Your account cannot be recovered
  • You will be logged out immediately
  • Any shared books will be unshared

If you need help with account deletion, contact us at etbookinfo@gmail.com
